About Lucky Shrub

Our story and our passion

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. What began as a shared passion has grown into Tucson's premier garden design firm.

Jason

Garden Architect

Creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers.

Maria

Marketing & Nursery Manager

Manages all marketing for the company and oversees the plant nursery.

Together, they've built Lucky Shrub into a one-stop shop for clients looking to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of".
